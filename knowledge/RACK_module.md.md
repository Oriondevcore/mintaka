🧠 What RACK RATE Actually Is

RACK RATE is a dynamic strategic control system that:

• Aligns revenue strategy with guest experience strategy
• Governs pricing aggression
• Influences upsell intensity
• Adjusts inventory strategy
• Quantifies deviation risk
• Links operational capacity with financial intent

It is not a price changer.

It is a decision accountability dashboard.

🎛 Core Interface Architecture
Primary Control: Strategic Slider

Horizontal gradient bar:

Gold (Left) → Max Revenue Per Guest
Teal (Right) → Max Guest Satisfaction

Default Position:
Balanced Growth (Satisfaction Leaning)

Slider outputs affect:

• ADR strategy
• Upsell frequency
• Promotional timing
• Inventory restriction intensity
• Overbooking tolerance

📊 Mathematical Engine (Under the Hood)

When slider moves, system recalculates:

Projected ADR
Projected OCC %
Projected RevPAR
Projected Revenue per Guest
Projected Satisfaction Score

Formula:

RevPAR \= ADR × OCC %

Upsell Intensity Index \= Slider Position × Elasticity Model

Satisfaction Risk Factor \= Aggression Coefficient × Historical Complaint Rate

Managers see trade-offs live.

🤖 Intelligence Mode Toggle

Below slider:

AI Recommendation (default)

Based on:

• Last 14 days performance
• Market demand signals
• Booking pace
• Historical seasonality
• Complaint frequency
• Conversion rate trends

AI Suggestion Example:
“Balanced Leaning – Demand strong but review sensitivity rising.”

🔁 Manual Override Mode

Manager can override AI.

When activated:

System displays:

Deviation Risk %
Revenue Volatility Score
Satisfaction Risk Indicator

Example:

⚠ 18% Deviation from AI Forecast
Projected Revenue Volatility: High
Projected Complaint Probability: \+6%

No restriction. Just transparency.

Accountability is visible.
