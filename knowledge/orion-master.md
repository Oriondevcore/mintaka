SECTION 2: THE MODULES
═══════════════════════════════════════════

--- MODULE 0: MAINTENANCE TICKETING ---
Purpose: Revenue Protection Layer
"Protect before you optimise. Revenue lost is harder to win back."

Broken things cause: Refunds, bad reviews, room downtime, lost bookings.
Fix speed = protected revenue.

Inputs: Room number, issue category, severity, timestamp, reported by
Severity: Low (cosmetic) / Medium (guest discomfort) / High (room unusable)

Key Metric: Room Downtime Recovery Rate (RDRR)
Formula: Downtime Reduction ÷ 1440 × ADR = Revenue Protected Per Room

Dashboard: Average Fix Time, Downtime %, Revenue Protected (ZAR), 7-Day Trend

Why start here: "No one argues with broken aircon. Measurable. Not threatening to staff."

--- MODULE 1: HOUSEKEEPING STATUS ---
Purpose: Accelerate Inventory (faster clean = earlier sellable room)

Key Metric: Turnover Velocity Index (TVI)
Sellable Hours Recovered (SHR) = Historical Average - Current Average

Dashboard: Average Turnover Time, Fastest Housekeeper, Rooms Ready Before 14:00,
Sellable Hours Recovered, Revenue Opportunity Gain (ZAR)

Managers love "Rooms Ready Before 14:00" — early check-ins = goodwill + upsell.

SECTION 4: HOSPITALITY GLOSSARY
═══════════════════════════════════════════

ADR    = Average Daily Rate (room revenue ÷ rooms sold)
RevPAR = Revenue Per Available Room (ADR × Occupancy %)
OCC%   = Occupancy % (rooms sold ÷ total rooms × 100)
ARR    = Average Room Rate
GOP    = Gross Operating Profit
NPS    = Net Promoter Score
PMS    = Property Management System (Opera, Nightsbridge, Protel)
POS    = Point of Sale (Micros, Gaap)
OTA    = Online Travel Agent (Booking.com, Expedia)
F&B    = Food and Beverage
HK     = Housekeeping
FO     = Front Office
GM     = General Manager
OOO    = Out of Order (room unavailable)
OOS    = Out of Service (temporarily unavailable)
Rack Rate = Published maximum rate (before discounts)
BAR    = Best Available Rate (dynamic pricing)
Comp   = Complimentary (free of charge)
Pax    = Number of people
Turndown = Evening housekeeping service

Check-in: Standard 14:00 | Check-out: Standard 10:00

SECTION 5: SOUTH AFRICAN CONTEXT
═══════════════════════════════════════════

Load Shedding (Eskom):
Stages 1-8. Higher = worse. Rotational power cuts.
Hotels need generator or solar for 3.5-star+.

Currency Rules (STRICT):
✅ R850 or "850 Rand" or "Eight hundred and fifty Rand"
❌ ZAR 850 / 850 ZAR / USD equivalent

Key Greetings:
Zulu: "Sawubona" (I see you) | Response: "Yebo, sawubona"
Xhosa: "Molo" (one person) | "Molweni" (group)
Afrikaans: "Hallo" / "Goeie dag"
