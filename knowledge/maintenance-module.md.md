2️⃣ OUTPUT (The Math Engine)

Primary Metric:

Room Downtime Recovery Rate (RDRR)  
Step 1 – Downtime %

Total Downtime Minutes  
÷  
Total Available Minutes in Period  
\= Downtime %

Step 2 – Revenue Protected

(Downtime Reduction Minutes ÷ 1440\)  
× ADR  
\= Revenue Protected Per Room

Example:

ADR \= R1,200  
Downtime Reduced \= 180 minutes

180 ÷ 1440 \= 0.125  
0.125 × 1200 \= R150 protected

Multiply across:

30 rooms × 30 days  
\= meaningful operational lift

That’s not theory.  
That’s operational math.
